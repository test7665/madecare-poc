Services

See how we're dealing with the coronavirus pandemic.

We pride ourselves in providing a range of services. These includes but not restricted to:

Domiciliary Care

We provide Home enablement service & assistance with daily living e.g: help with mobility, shower or bath, getting dressed, having breakfast e.t.c.

  • Shopping support – either alongside our client or independently with consent and agreed shopping list from our client.
  • Arrangement of Groceries.
  • Preparation of food and drink.
  • Ensuring a timely prompt for medication when due.
  • Medication administration for clients.
  • Undertaking domestic tasks such as cleaning, laundry and ironing either on a regular basis or as a ‘spring clean’, whatever the season.
  • At the end of each day, some help with getting ready for bed.
Live in care

Providing a care worker to live with you in your home. Similarly, we often provide live-in care when a customer needs temporary support through a period of convalescence or recuperation when returning from hospital.

Night care

Depending on your individual needs, our care package could include:

  • Provision of care services through the night for those people who need them. This can either be:
    • Sleeper night-where the care worker expects a reasonable night's sleep but is on hand for emergencies.
    • Waking night-where the care worker is on duty throughout the night
